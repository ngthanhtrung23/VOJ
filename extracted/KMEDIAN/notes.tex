Example
\begin{verbatim}
\textbf{Input:}
\\4 6 
\\10 
\\5 
\\6 
\\2 
\\
\\\textbf{Output:}
\\7 
\\
\\\end{verbatim}

\textbf{     Explain:    }    There are 10 possible contiguous subsequences to consider. Of these, only 7 have median at least 6. They are \{10\}, \{6\}, \{10, 5\}, \{5, 6\}, \{6, 2\}, \{10, 5, 6\}, \{10, 5, 6, 2\}.
